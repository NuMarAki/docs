Privacy Policy

Desmorda – Jaw Relaxation Reminders

1. Overview

Desmorda is a health-awareness app that helps users manage bruxism (teeth clenching) through periodic reminders and self-tracking. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is stored.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Desmorda does not require an account, login, or any personal information to function. The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any third-party data-collection services.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Pain/clenching records you create (timestamp, clenching yes/no, pain level 0–10)
  • Your preferences (reminder interval, sleep schedule, language, active state)

This data is stored using a local SQLite database and shared preferences. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it (CSV or PDF) and share the file yourself.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Exported reports (CSV/PDF) are generated locally and shared only through your device's share sheet at your discretion.

5. In-App Purchases

Desmorda offers an optional donation via Google Play In-App Billing. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive, store, or have access to any payment or financial information.

6. Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • Notifications – to deliver periodic jaw-relaxation reminders
  • Exact Alarms – to schedule reminders at precise intervals
  • Vibrate – to vibrate when a reminder fires
  • Boot Completed – to reschedule reminders after device restart

No permission is used to collect, transmit, or share user data.

7. Children's Privacy

Desmorda is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all records at any time from the app's Settings screen ("Clear data"). Uninstalling the app removes all stored data permanently.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
